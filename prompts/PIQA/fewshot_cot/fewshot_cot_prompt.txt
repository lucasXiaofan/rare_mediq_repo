### Instruction:
To permanently attach metal legs to a chair, you can A: Weld the metal together to get it to stay firmly in place, B: Nail the metal together to get it to stay firmly in place

### Response:
Option A, Welding, is the best choice for attaching metal legs permanently, as it fuses the metal parts securely. Welding creates a strong, stable bond but does require specialized equipment or professional assistance. Option B, Nailing, is ineffective for metal-to-metal attachments, as nails do not penetrate metal well and would not provide a durable hold. Therefore, welding is the suitable option for a secure, permanent attachment. The answer is: A: Weld the metal together to get it to stay firmly in place.

### Instruction:
Cameron decided to have a barbecue and gathered her friends together. How would Others feel as a result? A: like attending, B: like staying home, C: a good friend to have

### Response:
Let's think step by step.
Step 1: Cameron decided to organize a barbecue and invited her friends, which suggests she wants to create a welcoming, social event.
Step 2: Barbecues are typically enjoyable gatherings where friends come together to relax, eat, and have fun, which often makes people want to attend.
Step 3: This type of social setting generally attracts people rather than deterring them, as barbecues are seen as casual, friendly events.
Step 4: Given these points, it is reasonable to conclude that others would feel inclined to attend. The answer is A: like attending.
